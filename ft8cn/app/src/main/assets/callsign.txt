**About modifiers**
A CQ call may be followed by a modifier consisting of three decimal digits or one to four letters, usually in one of these forms: 000-999, A-Z, AA-ZZ, AAA-ZZZ, or AAAA-ZZZZ. For example: CQ POTA callsign grid.

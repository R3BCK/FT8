About audio output settings

Bit depth, also called sampling precision, refers to the level of detail used to quantize sound amplitude. In FT8CN, only 16-bit integer and 32-bit floating-point options are available. The higher the bit depth, the finer the amplitude resolution and the better the system can reproduce sound accurately.

Sample rate, also called sampling frequency, refers to the number of sound samples taken per second. A higher sample rate generally improves sound quality, but it also consumes more system resources.

**关于音频输出设置**

采样位深：也称采样精度，FT8CN只有16位整型和32位浮点可选。采样位深表示声音幅度量化后的精细程度，其数值越大，幅度分辨率越高，系统还原声音的能力越强。

采样率：也称取样频率，指每秒钟获取声音样本的次数。采样率越高，声音质量通常越好，但占用的资源也更多。
